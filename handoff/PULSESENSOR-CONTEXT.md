# PulseSensor Context

## Company

World Famous Electronics LLC makes PulseSensor products.

PulseSensor is an open, approachable heart-rate and PPG sensor ecosystem for makers, students, artists, educators, and researchers.

## Product DNA

PulseSensor is successful because it is:

- Simple.
- Visual.
- Arduino-friendly.
- Educational.
- Open and hackable.
- Easy to explain in a classroom.
- Small enough for beginner electronics workflows.

Do not destroy this simplicity when adding new features.

## Existing product value

PulseSensor is not only a sensor board. Its value includes:

- Brand trust.
- Documentation.
- Community familiarity.
- Educational examples.
- Arduino compatibility.
- Existing customer base.
- Known use in classrooms, labs, and maker projects.

## Ecosystem pieces

- PulseSensor hardware.
- Ring strap and finger mounting.
- Arduino examples.
- Processing visualizer.
- Browser / WebSerial demos.
- Shopify store.
- Amazon store.
- Educational content and tutorials.
- Book: Heartbeat Sensor Projects with PulseSensor.

## Current direction

Recent thinking favors going deeper on the original PulseSensor business and using Big Eye / PPG visualization work to expand the educational and maker ecosystem.

The strategy is to own the maker, classroom, and scientific-visualization niche for PPG.

## Product constraints

Future designs should protect:

- Beginner friendliness.
- Low support burden.
- Clear labels.
- Robust connectors.
- Easy examples.
- Manufacturability.
- Reasonable COGS.
- Clear product story.
